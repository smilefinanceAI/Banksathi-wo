Frequently Asked Questions

How to apply for a personal loan online? ▼
You can apply for a personal loan online by visiting our personal loan page, checking your eligibility, comparing interest rates from different banks, and submitting your application with basic documents like ID proof, address proof, and income proof.
What is the minimum CIBIL score required for a loan? ▼
Most banks require a minimum CIBIL score of 750 for personal loans. However, we have options for customers with scores as low as 650 through select lenders. Higher scores get better interest rates.
How much loan can I get on my salary? ▼
Generally, you can get a loan amount of 10-15 times your monthly salary. For example, if your monthly salary is ₹50,000, you may be eligible for a loan up to ₹5-7.5 lakhs. Use our eligibility calculator for exact amount.
Is there any processing fee for loans? ▼
Processing fees vary by bank and loan type, typically ranging from 0.5% to 2% of the loan amount. Some banks offer zero processing fee during festive seasons. Compare our offers to find the best deal.
